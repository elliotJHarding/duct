# The toolkit folder

Ticket folders come and go — sync rebuilds them from Jira. `toolkit/` is
the part that lasts: your workspace's config and accumulated knowledge,
tracked in its own git repo.

This is your `toolkit/`, exactly as it sits on disk:

$wiki_note

Because `toolkit/` is a git repo of its own, you can push and share it —
a teammate cloning it gets your workflow, agents, and conventions on day
one. The ticket folders around it never need backing up.
